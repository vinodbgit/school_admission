Welcome to Our School Where Excellence and Innovation Meet in Education.

To Provide a Quality Education For Brighter Future

At Shri D. K. Kotrashetti English Medium Lower Primary School,, we offer exceptional academic programs designed to meet the diverse needs of our students, ensuring they are well-prepared for future academic endeavors and career opportunities. Our experienced and passionate educators bring a wealth of knowledge and dedication to the classroom, fostering a supportive and stimulating learning environment that encourages student engagement and success. We boast state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, and extracurricular spaces, providing students with the resources they need to explore their interests and excel in their studies.


Facilities


Playground

Our well-equipped playground provides ample space and modern equipment for students to engage in various outdoor sports and physical activities.

Bus Facility

We offer safe and reliable bus transportation services for students, covering various routes to ensure convenience and accessibility for all.

Classroom

Our classrooms are designed to provide a conducive learning environment, equipped with modern teaching aids and comfortable seating arrangements.



Computer Lab

The computer lab features the latest technology and software, enabling students to develop essential digital literacy skills and engage in innovative projects.

Indoor Stadium

Our indoor stadium is designed for a variety of sports and physical activities, providing a space for students to develop their athletic skills in a controlled environment.

Swimming Pool

The swimming pool offers students the opportunity to learn and practice swimming, with trained instructors ensuring safety and skill development.
